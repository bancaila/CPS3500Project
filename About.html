Introduction

Major: Computational Science and Engineering

Computational Science and Engineering (CSE) is an interdisciplinary field that applies mathematical modeling, simulation, and computer science techniques to solve complex problems in science and engineering. CSE integrates various disciplines, including mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, engineering, and computer science, to create computational models and simulations that enable researchers to study and analyze complex systems. CSE has emerged as an essential tool for scientific research, allowing researchers to explore phenomena that are impossible to study experimentally, leading to breakthroughs in various scientific fields.
